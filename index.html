about us

Welcome to Dingsutan!

We, the DingSutan team, proudly present a high-quality milk and coconut pudding that will tantalize your taste buds with a unique blend of deliciousness and creaminess. DingSutan is born out of our obsession to create an unforgettable pudding experience.

Here, we seamlessly blend authentic flavors with modern innovation to deliver a product that is exceptional and satisfying. Each cup of DingSutan is filled with the smoothness of premium quality milk, coupled with the fragrant and delightful essence of coconut milk. We prioritize fresh and high-quality ingredients, along with a meticulous production process, to ensure the best quality in every serving of DingSutan.
